About

Hi! My name is Michael Rudloff aka "Cole Modelo." I'm a full-time freelance software engineer and part-time DJ based out of Denver, CO. Since an early age, I've always been curious about computers and instruments. Over the years, that curiousity has grown into a love for web development and live sound. Eventually leading me to where I am today.

I am a big fan of community driven projects and open-source software as well as exploring new browsers, programs, and technologies. In my spare time, I like to go mountain-biking, explore new places, and collect records amongst many other things.

My technical skills include: HTML/CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, React, Git, Node.js, Jest, Gimp, Figma, Serato, and MongoDB.
